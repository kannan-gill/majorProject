Project Report
Need of the Project

This project can be used to perform dip coating on a substrate at a much cheaper rate as compared to the market available options. Dip coating is very important step in electronics and for a lot of companies and laboratories, the main blocking factor in making their own substrate and conducting research by varying parameters is the high cost of dip coater. This project targets to resolve that issue and encourage the manufacture and testing of various properties on the thickness of coating on a substrate by giving them a much cheaper alternate.

Features
  • - Highest quality coating with minimal vibrations.
  • - Portable design allows to use Dip coater in labs.
  • - Variable speed and delay helps conduct various experiments for research purposes.
  • - Logs of conducted experiments and a dedicated website.
  • - Cheap alternative to current dip coaters present in market.
How to run Dip Coater

The 2 variables that we have ie speed and delay can be entered by 2 different ways.

  • - Enter the speed and delay manually with the help of keypad and lcd screen on the device.
  • - Use website for entering speed and delay and use Dip Coater through Wifi.
  • - For using via website, go to Fabricate in menu of website and add speed, delay values. Click Fabricate and see the results.
  • - Once the experiment is done, user can go to Research column and see conducted experiments there. One can also add the observed thickness against the serial number of experiment to maintain logs.
